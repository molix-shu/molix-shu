## 🚀 代表项目

### 1. 基于差分隐私和 LSTM 的车联网轨迹隐私保护系统

该项目面向车联网轨迹数据隐私保护场景，结合 **差分隐私** 与 **LSTM 虚假轨迹生成** 方法，实现对车辆轨迹数据的保护、发布与评估。

**技术关键词：**

`Differential Privacy` `LSTM` `Trajectory Data` `IoV` `Flask` `Vue` `ECharts` `Leaflet`

**主要功能：**

- 基于空间语义风险的自适应隐私预算分配
- 对轨迹经纬度数据进行 Laplace 噪声扰动
- 使用 LSTM 生成虚假轨迹，构建混淆路径集合
- 支持原始轨迹与保护轨迹的地图可视化对比
- 提供隐私性与可用性综合评估指标

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1. IoV Trajectory Privacy Protection System Based on Differential Privacy and LSTM

This project focuses on trajectory privacy protection in the Internet of Vehicles.  
It combines **Differential Privacy** and **LSTM-based fake trajectory generation** to protect, publish, and evaluate vehicle trajectory data.

**Keywords:**

`Differential Privacy` `LSTM` `Trajectory Data` `IoV` `Flask` `Vue` `ECharts` `Leaflet`

**Main Features:**

- Adaptive privacy budget allocation based on spatial semantic risk
- Laplace noise perturbation for trajectory coordinates
- LSTM-based fake trajectory generation
- Original vs. protected trajectory visualization
- Privacy and utility evaluation metrics
